Introduction

This section is dedicated to the Users, Players, and Referees tables in the Fulvo Database. The reason for combining these three tables is that they are all related to each other in some way. For example, in the Users Table, there are two types of roles: Player and Referee. The Player and Referee tables are related to the Users table through a foreign key constraint.
